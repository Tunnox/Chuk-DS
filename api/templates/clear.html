CLEAR Metadata Framework

Developed by Chukwuemeka Oluwaseun Umunna, August 2025

The CLEAR Framework helps ensure your metadata is clear, complete, discoverable, and aligned with generally accepted guidance. Use the guidance below and complete the quick questionnaire to assess the quality of your metadata.

CLEAR Guidance Summary

  • C – Clarity: Plain English, understandable, avoids unexplained jargon, describes the dataset not the project.
  • L – Length & Focus: Titles ≤ 60 characters; abstracts ≥ 100 characters, with the first 150 summarising key info.
  • E – Essential Information: Include What, Where, When (title) + What, Where, When, How, Why, Who (abstract).
  • A – Accuracy: No repeated text, correct acronym formatting, accurate dataset description.
  • R – Readability: Sentence case, natural writing, enough context to judge relevance.
